Birthday Parties

We provide the plates, napkins, cups, forks, and fun. You bring the kids and the cake! Please email info@flipcity.biz to register.

Parties will begin with 60 minutes of play time in the gym led by a Flip City staff member, followed by 30 minutes in the party room for cake and presents.

$160 for up to 10 kids / $10 per additional child.

Currently enrolled students receive a 15% discount
