Flip City's Mission

Flip City Gymnastics aims to promote growth and learning through the process and experience of gymnastics training. Students develop a strong work ethic with an ability to set and reach goals. Gymnasts are taught and expected to show respect and compassion toward themselves, their peers, and their coaches.

What is my motivation?

I was introduced to gymnastics in 1996 watching the Magnificent Seven win the first ever Gold Medal for the United States in the Women's Team Competition at the Atlanta Olympics. I watched these young girls who were focused and dedicated to the most amazing, beautiful sport I had ever seen and I instantly wanted to be like them. Their achievements made me realize that I didn't have to wait until I reached adulthood to do great things. I likely would never go to the Olympics, but I was plenty capable of being strong, focused, and disciplined like the girls I watched on television.

Once I started learning gymnastics, I did everything I could to get better at it as quickly as possible. At 10 years old, I was doing 300 sit-ups and 100 pushups each night, not because anyone told me to but because I wanted to get better at gymnastics. I had something to work for. I had a reason to do more than watch television all summer. Soon enough, I was showing off back walkovers and front handsprings to my family and friends. I realized that I could do skills that most other people, adults included, could not. I gained a confidence that I had not had before. Each skill I mastered gave me one more accomplishment of which I could be proud. I saw where hard work could lead me and that lesson motivated me throughout adolescence, college, and into adulthood.

This is what I strive to teach kids every day. Being young does not mean being incapable of doing great things. Through gymnastics, kids learn to set and achieve goals. Each skill mastered is a tangible accomplishment through which they can see their progress and develop confidence in their ability to achieve whatever goals they may choose in life.
